YUMEKO JABAMI

Yumeko Jabami is the protagonist of the anime manga Kakegurui. She is a transfer student from Hyakkao private academy; Yumeko is a very cheerful and charismatic character who constantly attracts the affection of the people around her.

She has a severe problem with gambling, She is very skilled and a strategist, so she always manages to figure out a way out when she bets; her purpose is to look for betting games that make her feel alive
